**KAIST** | 2023

CODA is the algorithm for counting directed hypergraphlets on directed hypergraphs.
- Define novel local patterns on directed hypergraphs (directed hypergraphlets)
- Design efficient algorithms: CODA-E(xact), CODA-A(dvanced)
- Applications: clustering, classification, and temporal analysis
