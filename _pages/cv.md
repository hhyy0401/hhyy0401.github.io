## Research Experiences

- **EEG Analysis for Cognitive and Clinical Modeling** -- Georgia Tech _(Sep. 2024 --)_
  Drift-Diffusion Model (DDM) analysis of EEG signals for characterizing decision-making processes.
  EEG-based disease prediction leveraging neural biomarkers and deep learning methods.

- **fMRI-driven Graph Modeling of Self-Organization Mechanisms in the Visual Cortex** -- Georgia Tech _(Sep. 2024 --)_
  Develop graph generation methods using both AI-driven approaches and rule-based algorithms.
  Design brain-inspired models by leveraging neural data visualization mechanisms [P1, P2].

- **Algorithm, Analysis, and Application of Higher-Order Structures** -- KAIST _(Mar. 2022 -- Feb. 2024)_
  Designed scalable algorithms for counting and characterizing higher-order structures (simplets, hypergraphlets, motifs)
  in simplicial complexes, hypergraphs, and directed hypergraphs.
  Developed sampling-based estimation methods with provable accuracy guarantees [C1, J1, J2, J3].

- **Development of a Null Model for GNN Incorporating Node Features** -- KAIST _(May 2023 -- Dec. 2023)_
  Developed a null model maintaining performance rankings aligned with the original network
  while anonymizing users' information.

- **Performance Prediction of Architectures using Semi-Supervised Learning** -- KAIST _(Apr. 2022 -- Dec. 2023)_
  Neural architecture performance prediction method using flow-aware Graph Transformer.
  Registered two South Korea Copyrights for Software (C-2022-055775, C-2023-047128).
  Patent filed: KR 10-2024-0047575 (filed 2024) [C2].
  Supported by IITP grant funded by the Korea government (MSIT).
